Specialized Health Checkups

At Kalika Enterprises, we offer a range of specialized health checkups tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our comprehensive health assessment programs are designed to provide thorough evaluations and screenings, ensuring early detection of potential health issues and promoting overall well-being.

Our specialized health checkups include:

  • Cardiovascular Health Checkup

    Our cardiovascular health checkups include tests such as cholesterol levels, blood pressure monitoring, and electrocardiograms (ECG) to assess heart function and detect any signs of heart disease or related conditions.

  • Metabolic Health Assessment

    These checkups focus on evaluating metabolic health indicators such as blood sugar levels, lipid profiles, and body composition analysis to identify risks associated with diabetes, obesity, and metabolic syndrome.

  • Cancer Screening

    We offer comprehensive cancer screening packages that include tests such as mammograms, Pap smears, prostate-specific antigen (PSA) tests, and colonoscopies to detect early signs of cancer and facilitate timely intervention.

  • Genetic Testing

    Genetic testing involves examining a person's blood or other tissues to determine whether he or she has a change in his or her genetic material. Genetic testing may be useful in determining whether an individual has a genetic condition or may develop one in the future.

  • Immunological Evaluation

    Immunology provides testing and advice to support the diagnosis, investigation, and monitoring of allergic disease, autoimmune/connective tissue disease, immunodeficiency, monoclonal gammopathies, and related disorders.

  • Neurological Assessment

    Our nutritional health checkups involve assessing dietary habits, nutrient levels, and metabolic parameters to provide personalized recommendations for optimizing nutrition and promoting overall well-being.

Each health checkup is conducted by experienced healthcare professionals utilizing state-of-the-art diagnostic tools and technologies. We prioritize accuracy, confidentiality, and personalized care to ensure the highest level of service for our clients.
